Divide is a number based game that involves challenging of your division and multiplication skills. You get exciting, unpredictable yet well design sequence of numbers to test your skills. The more you play, the better you will be in the game. It starts with easy numbers and increases the toughness as you play more.

Can you cross all the levels even with unlimited tries? Play! It is a challenge for you. Play the game once and you will never put it down.

How to play?

There are two modes of the game: 3x3 and 4x4. In each mode, you will get a matrix of the respective size e.g. in 3x3 mode, you get a matrix of 3 rows and 3 columns.

In 3x3, There are 9 blocks in the game. You get numbers in a queue. You have to arrange each number in any of the blocks. The rule is, if there are two numbers adjacent to each other, it divides each other if one number is divisible by another number. You score points when you place such numbers in the block. The goal is to score as much as possible without running out of space. If you are running out of spaces, you can use it keep feature to store extra number. There is no time limit in the game.
